This is about The Foundation Trilogy

The first four stories were collected, along with a new introductory story, and published by Gnome Press in 1951 as Foundation.

The later stories were published in pairs by Gnome as Foundation and Empire (1952) and Foundation (1953),

resulting in the "Foundation Trilogy", as the series is still known.[6]
